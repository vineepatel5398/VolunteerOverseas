Duration

Start Dates

2Apply Now

Follow our easy and secure application process:

1

Fill out your trip preferences

2

Tell your program why you are a good fit

3

Reserve your spot with a small deposit*

*This deposit is generally a small portion of the total project fee. Your deposit secures your spot with the program and shows that you are a serious applicant. This deposit is fully refundable if you are not accepted by program.

3Wait to Hear Great News

After you submit your application, you will hear from your program within 3-7 business days.

$

Raise funds for your trip on Volunteer Forever's no-fee crowdfunding platform
